## Introduction

We offer OpenAI's voices and 11Labs voices. The 11Labs AI model produces the highest-quality AI voices in the industry.

The 11Labs text-to-speech model allows you to convert text into audio in 29 languages and 1000s of voices. Integrate our realistic text to speech voices into your react app, use our Python library or our websockets guide to get started.
